Key Features

Personalized Home Page

Upon launching the app, users are greeted with a personalized home page that displays a list of their connected friends. Each friend’s name is prominently featured alongside an emoji that reflects their current emotional state, allowing for quick insights into how they are doing.

Emoji Status Updates

Users can select from a curated set of key emojis to represent their mood or well-being. This intuitive system makes it easy for friends to communicate their feelings without the need for lengthy messages, promoting a culture of openness and support.

Tribe Awareness

The app enables users to see an overview of their tribe's emotional statuses at a glance. By identifying friends who might be having a tough day, users can take proactive steps to reach out and offer support, whether that’s through a call, text, or in-person visit.

Seamless Communication

TribeUp encourages meaningful interactions among friends. With just a tap, you can initiate a phone call or send a message to a friend who seems to need a little extra love, strengthening the bonds within their community of family and friends.

Customizable Notifications

Stay updated with customizable notifications that alert you whenever a friend updates their emoji status. This feature ensures that you’re always in the loop and can seize the opportunity to check in on someone who may need it.

Mental Health Advocate Connection

For users who are struggling or have concerns about a friend, TribeUp offers a direct connection to mental health advocates and counselors. Users can reach out for guidance and support, ensuring they have access to professional help when needed.

Mental Health Resources

TribeUp provides a curated library of mental health resources, including articles, hotlines, and self-help tools. Users can access:

  • Crisis Hotlines: Direct links to local and national crisis hotlines for immediate support.
  • Educational Articles: Informative content on mental health topics, coping strategies, and wellness tips.
  • Self-Help Tools: Interactive tools and exercises aimed at improving emotional well-being.

Quick Access Widget

For added convenience, TribeUp includes a handy widget for your iPhone home screen. This widget provides a quick glance at your tribe’s statuses, making it easier than ever to stay connected without opening the app.

Why Choose TribeUp?

In an age where digital communication often lacks depth, TribeUp bridges the gap by fostering genuine connections. Whether it's a friend having a challenging day or simply a reminder to check in, this app helps cultivate a supportive network. Join the TribeUp community today and make emotional well-being a priority for yourself and those you care about.
